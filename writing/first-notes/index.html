systems notes

Joyboydata is a small lab for building and writing about modern data systems. The work is a mix of architecture experiments, careful tooling choices, and the occasional contrarian take when common patterns feel too heavy.

What you can expect here:

  • Practical notes on data modeling, segmentation, and activation
  • Postmortems on decisions that worked (and the ones that did not)
  • Lightweight tooling patterns that respect constraints and focus

If you want updates, the RSS feed is the most reliable way to follow along.
